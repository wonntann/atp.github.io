Logo

Logo Design
Project Description

Mimesis is an innovative open source library for Python programming language that generates mock data in various languages for a variety of purposes. This data is often called for during software development and testing. Mimesis' original logo of an octopus embodied the purpose of the data, so we did not want to deviate too far from the original symbol. However, Mimesis needed a fresh, minimal logo to freshen up the current logo.

Target Audience

Technology benefits individuals and companies in uncountable ways, and must regularly evolve in order to meet these demands. Data is a crucial step in developing cutting-edge technology. Market research shows that consumers expect.

Results

The clean, geometric lines shows authority and professionalism, while the overall shape remains an octopus. The unique and simple design separates itself from the competition while matching the branding image that Mimesis is trying to create.
