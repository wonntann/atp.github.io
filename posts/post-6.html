Designed the pizzeria website for clientele that wanted to order their pizza's in one click after looking at the designed menu. After correctly filling out the Delivery Information, you are directed to place your custom pizza order in a streamlined order form using JavaScript.

I developed a real-time order form coded with JavaScript, along with the design of the menu and logo, and the website layout.
