A Little About Me

Your business will find positive benefits when incorporating accessibility.

In this digital age, navigation should not be the prevention of accessing your business online. I am trying to eliminate barriers in information technology which cultivates inclusion and equity. Accessibility enables everyone to see, hear, and simply use technology to engage independently. For many people with limitations, whether a language barrier, congenital or acquired, or temporary or permanent, accessibility is what makes using the web obtainable.


I strive to foster ideas of inclusion, accessibility and equity. In turn, the elimination of barriers for different audiences, will open your business to a wider community. My passion is with enabling people and businesses to realize their full potential. I guarantee this promise by working alongside your business creating informational technology that is accessible to everyone -of all ages, abilities and backgrounds. I reach for making the world a more inclusive home setting by widening the parameters of accessibility in the information technology realm.


I work with your business to expand it’s horizons. Enabling accessibility online welcomes a wider audience and creates inclusion. I believe that this will give your customers the respect and independence they rightly deserve.

Help me advance accessibility and promote the independence of the members within your community in a caring and compassionate manner.
